Case study

Operations Dashboard Revamp

How we consolidated alerts, analytics and audit into a single real‑time dashboard, improving incident response and executive visibility.

Challenge

Fragmented tooling meant slow MTTR and no unified view across customer impact, infra metrics, and business KPIs.

Solution

Event streaming, rule‑based alerting, and a unified dashboard with RBAC. Historical replay and drill‑downs for root cause analysis.

Outcomes

  • MTTR reduced by 38%
  • Executive NOC board with KPI overlays
  • Audit logs for compliance and handoffs

Architecture highlights

Ingestion

Kafka streams from services and 3rd‑party tools.

Processing

Stream processors for rules and correlation.

Storage

Time‑series DB for metrics, OLAP for analytics.

Experience

React dashboard with drill‑downs and export.
