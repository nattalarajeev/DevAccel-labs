Business Plans: Strategy to Execution

How next‑forge helped a scaling company formalize business planning across finance, product, and operations—turning strategy into measurable outcomes.

Challenge

Planning lived in slides and spreadsheets with poor alignment and no real‑time tracking. Quarterly goals slipped and budget ownership was unclear.

Solution

Introduced goal hierarchies (Company → Dept → Squad), workflow approvals, and live dashboards. Budgets, initiatives, and KPIs linked in a single system.

Outcomes

  • Quarterly planning cycle time reduced by 45%
  • Budget variance alerting and audit trails
  • Cross‑functional accountability with OKR progress in real‑time

What’s inside the Business Plans template

OKRs & Initiatives

Define objectives, align initiatives, and track progress.

Budget & Forecasts

Plan spend, compare actuals, and flag variance automatically.

Roadmap & Dependencies

Visualize delivery timelines and upstream/downstream risks.

Reporting

Executive dashboards, exports, and stakeholder digests.
